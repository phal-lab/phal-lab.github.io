Dr. Yamuna Phal has been selected as a NASA Glenn Faculty Fellow and will spend the summer collaborating with the Photonics Division at NASA Glenn Research Center in Cleveland. The fellowship advances joint research in photonic instrumentation and mid-infrared sensing technologies for space-based and aerospace applications, further expanding Phal Lab’s growing portfolio of NASA partnerships.
